## COPY NOTES FOR CLAUDE DESIGN

### Tone & Voice

- **Compassionate** — acknowledge the weight of caregiving  
- **Direct** — no flowery language, speak to real concerns  
- **Action-oriented** — always point toward a next step  
- **Warm but professional** — not clinical, not overly casual

### Key Messages

1. **You don't have to do this alone** — core emotional promise  
2. **We understand your situation** — specific to adult children, aging parents  
3. **In-home care is possible** — demystify the option  
4. **Peace of mind is achievable** — emotional outcome, not just services  
5. **No pressure to commit** — remove friction from exploration

### Copy Patterns (Apply Throughout)

- **Use short sentences** — digestible, not overwhelming  
- **Repetition for emphasis** — "You want to know… You want less… You want to stop…"  
- **Real-world language** — "forgetting things", "getting harder", not medical jargon  
- **Italic accents in headlines** — emphasize 1-2 key words (design system: \#73726c color)

### Button CTAs (Consistency)

- Primary CTA: "Book Your Free Care Consultation" (main conversion)  
- Secondary CTAs: "Talk To A Care Specialist", "Let's Talk", "Find The Right Care"  
- All buttons use SecFi design system: dark pill (\#292a2d, \#ffffff text, radius 300px)

### Section Breakdown (for Claude Design layout)

1. Hero (2-column, image right)  
2. Trust strip (single line, centered)  
3. Problem (text block \+ bullets)
